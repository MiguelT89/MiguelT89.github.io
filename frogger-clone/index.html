Frogger Clone Game

How to Play

  1. Select your desired character from the list to the right and enter it's number into the prompt. If you ever want to change your character's avatar you'll have to refresh the page.
  2. Use the arrows keys move your character up, down, left or right.
  3. Your goal is to reach the water without being squished by the bugs.
